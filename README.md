## How to Test This Yourself Right Now

You don't need to buy 10 different Android phones to verify that the UI is safe. You can test it directly on your computer:

- **Resize the Window:** If you are running the app on the Windows/Web emulator, simply grab the edge of the application window with your mouse and drag it to make it extremely narrow, then extremely wide. You will watch the Flutter widgets dynamically squish, expand, and drop to new lines in real-time.
- **Change Emulator Devices:** In Android Studio or VS Code, you can switch your virtual device from a large standard phone (like a Pixel 7 Pro) to a tiny, older screen size (like a Pixel 3a or Nexus 4). Boot it up, and you will see the layout adapt perfectly.
